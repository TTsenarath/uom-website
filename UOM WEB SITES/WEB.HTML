    • Welcome to Sri Lanka

    Sri Lanka, a nation on the Indian Ocean, is well-known for its diverse animals and abundant biodiversity. Sri Lanka's turtle population is one of the country's most recognizable and adored species of animal. There are five different kinds of sea turtles that can be found in Sri Lanka. In marine ecology, these turtles are significant.

    Sri Lankan turtle populations face a variety of problems, including poaching, unintentional capture in fishing nets, pollution, habitat destruction, and changes in the climate, despite their ecological significance and their endangered status.
